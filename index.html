about us :

When Adwaa Al-Hikma was established back in 2009, many values were taken into consideration to ensure the future success of the company. Values that depict a company that can improve the delivery of care across the healthcare chain. Our office specializes in the field of medical supplies, medical and laboratory diagnostic devices, laboratory solutions and optics. The company's mission is to provide all medical and laboratory equipment and supplies, surgical materials and general materials of international origin in order to contribute to the development of the health sector in the southern region and Iraq to a level that rises to the global health sector.
